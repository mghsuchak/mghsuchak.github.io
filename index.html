Megha Suchak (Henna designer in SF)

Megha Suchak is the founder of hennainsf.com. She grew up Ahmedabad, India and currently lives in San Francisco, California. She is fluent in Gujarati, Hindi as well as English. Megha has a bachelors degree in Information and Technology.

Although she is a versatile artist who is comfortable with different styles of designs, her favorites are Traditional Indian and Arabic designs.
